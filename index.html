Become a Pokémon Master.

The main staple of becoming a Pokémon trainer revolves around catching and battling of Pokémon. Trainers can catch wild Pokémon by weakening them and catching them with Poké Balls. Conversely, they can choose to defeat them in battle in order to gain experience for their Pokémon, raising their levels and teaching them new moves. Here, at Charlie's Training Camp, you will obtain the knowledge and skill to confidently take on these challenges.

Your first Pokémon



Starter Pokémon, or Starters, are the Pokémon a trainer chooses at the beginning of their Pokémon journey.
We have carefully selected 3 species suitable for the tasks but the rest of their development will depend on you.




Bulbasaur

It bears the seed of a plant on its back from birth. The seed slowly develops. Bulbasaur are extremely calm and very difficult to capture in the wild.
